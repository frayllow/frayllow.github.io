LAVENDER

caution or calmness

I remember those times na I notice u staring deeply sa ako. I always thought na “Why is he looking at me like that? Scary lagi ni siya”. Your gazes sent chills down my spine. It made me think does he want to murder me or

does he like me?

Who would’ve thought na scary kayka sa ako sauna but now you’re one of the people who bring calmness in my life; my safe space.

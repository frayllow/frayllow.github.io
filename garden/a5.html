Chrysanthemum indicum L.

05

RED CHRYSANTHEMUM

i love you

In the last days of June, I told you about being scared because I might not be able to equal your love for me. I didn’t want to disappoint you, but telling you to end what’s going on between us hurt me more than I expected. Joan even dubbed you as my TOTGA when I had to come to her for comfort.

To be honest, when I started talking again to you, I still somewhat felt that the cruel words I said to you were true, but this time I had gambled my feelings and also yours. This would have either end in a happy relationship or a painful disaster. I’m so sorry for taking such a huge risk, but now I have no regrets.

There are no more doubts on what I feel for you, nor do I think they are any lesser than yours.
